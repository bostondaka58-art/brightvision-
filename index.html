About Bright Vision

A Lusaka-born creative studio built on passion, precision, and a relentless belief that African stories deserve world-class production.

BRIGHT VISION
2014
Est. Lusaka, ZM

A Studio Built on Bright Ideas

Bright Vision Multimedia was founded in 2014 with a simple but powerful conviction: every brand has a story worth telling beautifully. What started as a small video production outfit in Lusaka has grown into a full-service multimedia studio trusted by businesses across Zambia and beyond.

We are storytellers, designers, builders, and audio creators. Our team brings together a rare blend of artistic talent and strategic thinking — which is why the work we produce doesn't just look great, it performs.

From multinational campaigns to grassroots brands finding their voice, we approach every project with the same level of care, craft, and commitment to your vision.

Our Journey

10 Years of Bright Vision

2014 — Founded
Bright Vision Multimedia launched in Lusaka with two cameras, three passionate creatives, and a dream.
2016 — Graphic Design Studio
Launched our dedicated design division, taking on brand identity and print work for Zambian SMEs.
2019 — Web & Digital Expansion
Added a web design and development arm, building digital experiences for clients across sectors.
2021 — Podcast Studio Opens
Opened Zambia's first dedicated brand podcast studio, helping organisations launch and grow their audio presence.
2024 — 200+ Projects
Hit our 200-project milestone with a team of 14 creatives, working with clients across Zambia, Zimbabwe, and Kenya.
